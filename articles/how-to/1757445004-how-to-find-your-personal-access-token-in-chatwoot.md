# How to Find Your Personal Access Token in Chatwoot

Chatwoot provides a comprehensive set of free, publicly available APIs for all users. You can explore our complete API documentation [here](https://developers.chatwoot.com/api-reference/introduction).

To make API calls to your Chatwoot account, you'll need a Personal Access Token for authentication. This guide will walk you through the process of locating your access token.

> **Important Note**: You must have administrator privileges on your account to access your Personal Access Token.

## Step 1: Access Your Profile

Click on your **avatar image** in the bottom left corner of the screen to open your profile menu.

## Step 2: Open Profile Settings

From the profile menu, select **"Profile Settings"** to view all your profile configuration options.

![](https://app.chatwoot.com/rails/active_storage/blobs/redirect/eyJfcmFpbHMiOnsibWVzc2FnZSI6IkJBaHBCR3FyTkFJPSIsImV4cCI6bnVsbCwicHVyIjoiYmxvYl9pZCJ9fQ==--184e91d510f214756bc419c64c35f40dc466db1e/step-2.png)

## Step 3: Locate Your Access Token

Scroll to the bottom of the Profile Settings page to find your **Personal Access Token**.

![](https://app.chatwoot.com/rails/active_storage/blobs/redirect/eyJfcmFpbHMiOnsibWVzc2FnZSI6IkJBaHBCS1NyTkFJPSIsImV4cCI6bnVsbCwicHVyIjoiYmxvYl9pZCJ9fQ==--1444d11759c54b0b16697fa045cd4fa40483848e/last-step.png)

## Using Your Access Token

Once you've located your token:

1. **Copy** the access token

2. Include it in your API request headers for successful authentication

3. Keep your token secure and never share it publicly

Your Personal Access Token serves as your unique identifier for all API interactions with your Chatwoot account, so treat it with the same security as you would a password.
