## Key Metrics

- **Conversation volume**: Total created, resolved, pending
- **Response times**: First response, next response, resolution time
- **CSAT score**: Customer satisfaction rating (1-5)
- **SLA breach rate**: % of conversations breaching SLA
- **Agent workload**: Conversations per agent, resolution rate
- **Bot resolution rate**: % handled without human intervention

## Best Practices

- Set up SLA policies before relying on SLA reports
- Review CSAT reports weekly to identify trends
- Use conversation reports to spot peak hours and staffing needs
- Filter by team, inbox, or label for granular analysis
